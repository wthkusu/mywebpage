Exploring the Head Section of Amazon Music

1. og:title
This meta tag is part of the Open Graph protocol. It controls the title that appears when the website link is shared on social media platforms.

2. og:image
This meta tag specifies the image that appears when the website is shared on social media.

3. twitter:card
This tag tells Twitter how to display the link preview. "summary_large_image" shows a large image when the link is shared.
